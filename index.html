About (CV)

I am currently an Associate Professor in College of Computer Science and Technology, Qingdao University, Qingdao, China. I am also with the Institute of Artifical Intelligent .

I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2015, under the supervision of Prof Lin Weisi. I obtained my BS and MS degrees from Heilongjiang Institute of Technology, China, in 2007 and Shandong University, China, in 2010, respectively.

My current research interests include image/video processing and analysis, perception-based modeling and quality assessment, object detection/recognition, and machine learning.

I am looking for highly motivated Master students with strong background in CS, EE and Math. Undergraduate students who are interested in my research are also welcome to join us as research assistant. Feel free to join us if your are interested.
